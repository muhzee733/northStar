Our Story

Fire Alarm Solutions with Over 25 Years of Experience

NorthStar Systems was founded by Toby Woods, a seasoned expert with over 25 years of experience in the fire alarm industry. Toby's career began in the submarine service, where he learned the critical importance of attention to detail and precision. These values have carried over into every aspect of his work in the fire alarm industry. Having worked across various roles, including installation, system planning, and project management, Toby has developed a deep understanding of the industry's complexities and the importance of creating safe environments.
